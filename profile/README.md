## Welcome

Computations for Orthopedics and Biomechanics (COB) is a research team at the Washington State University Applied Sciences Laboratory on the Health Sciences campus in Spokane.
We specialize in modeling and simulation of orthopedic mechanics with particular focus on the knee.

## Modeling and Simulation Workflows

Defining a model and performing simulations is just one stage in a modeling and simulation workflow.
